Welcome to Them Trails

We are a community of trail runners who love to explore the great outdoors. Whether you're a seasoned trail runner or just starting out, we have something for everyone. Join us for group runs two times a week or participate in one of our many events throughout the year. We are a LGBTQIQA+ and allies community offering a safe environment for trail runners. So lace up your shoes, grab your hydration pack, and let's hit the trails together!
